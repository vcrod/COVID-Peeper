COVID-19 Facts

Nine quick facts about COVID-19:

  1. COVID-19 aka Coronavirus, is an illness caused by a virus that spreads easily from person to person.
  2. COVID-19 is a GLOBAL pandemic.
  3. People of all age groups can become infected.
  4. Symptoms can range from mild or no symptoms to severe illness or death.
  5. You can become infected from respiratory droplets when an infected person coughs, sneezes, or by simply having a conversation about how poorly your country has handled the pandemic!
  6. People with absolutely no symptoms can spread the virus.
  7. Touching anything with the virus on it and then touching your eyes, nose, or mouth can infect you.
  8. The virus can live on surfaces for days.
  9. Simple actions like properly wearing a face mask and hand washing can help stop the spread of COVID-19.
